Frequently asked questions

What counts as one video?

Each YouTube or Rumble URL submitted for processing counts as one video. Free accounts get 3 per month, Pro is unlimited.

What output formats do I get?

Structured JSON (transcripts, Q&As, summaries, key topics), downloadable PDFs, and plain text — ready to drop into any LLM fine-tuning pipeline.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel immediately from the Stripe billing portal. Your Pro access stays active until the end of the billing period.

Do you support long videos?

Yes — up to 2 hours per video. Most tutorial and lecture videos fall well within that limit.

Is there an API?

Pro subscribers get REST API access to submit videos and retrieve results programmatically. Documentation available after subscribing.
